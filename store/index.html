STORE

The downeast community thrift store was established in 2014 to sell low cost items to the community and use the profits to help people with emergency financial crisis. In addition, the women in the downeast women's shelter can shop for clothing, shoes, and household items they may need.

The thrift store is run by volunteers and is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit business and ministry. The donations come from local residents, churches, and many visitors bring donations to our community store.

We graciously accept new or gently used donation items including:

  • Furniture
  • Clothing
  • Toys
  • Books
  • Housewares

Follow us on Facebook for updates and information on new items!

Downeast Community Thrift Store

Our vision is to provide food, clothing, shelter, and emergency funds for the homeless, helpless, and hopeless of Carteret County in partnership with other outreach organizations.
