WOMEN'S SHELTER

The downeast women's shelter was donated to the community in 2016. The shelter has 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms which is ideal to shelter women. The women come from all over the state and are referred many times by other shelters or agencies who have a time limit for their residents. The shelter can provide housing and assistance to residents for up to one year. The women are mentored and encouraged as they seek employment, transportation and housing. Ezra Ministries partners with many local agencies to better serve the community.

2016-2018 by the Numbers

15 women housed
4 women employed
2 in higher education
4 receiving medical treatment

The Farm

In 2017, we were able to open Ezra Farms along with our women's shelter. Eight women were sheltered and worked on the farm to heal, restore, and experience peace through nature.

How to Help

We are only limited by the amount of resources available to meet the needs of the women in our shelter. We trust the Lord to provide, as He has already, and He has used generous people like you who want to invest in others.

We have found that shelters are a very expensive, and a resource dependent outreach. Currently, we need a van, a water softener, new siding, and an increase in monthly support for 6 women.

Our vision is to provide food, clothing, shelter, and emergency funds for the homeless, helpless, and hopeless of Carteret County in partnership with other outreach organizations.
