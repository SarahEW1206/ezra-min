WOMEN'S SHELTER

The downeast women's shelter was donated to the community in 2016. The shelter has 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms which is ideal to shelter women. The women come from all over the state and are referred many times by other shelters or agencies who have a time limit for their residents. The shelter can provide housing and assistance to residents for up to one year. The women are mentored and encouraged as they seek employment, transportation and housing. Ezra Ministries partners with many local agencies to better serve the community.

2016-2018 by the Numbers

15 women housed
4 women employed
2 in higher education
4 receiving medical treatment
